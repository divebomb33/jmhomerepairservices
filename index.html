About

JM Home Repair Services© is a multi faceted full service construction company. Our company is owner operated and has been very successful in part to our "customers come first" philosophy! We specialize in all aspects of the building trades and are especially versed in water damage restoration work. JM Home Repair Services offers services to the South Shore and Greater Boston Area.

Founder and CEO Jim Minkle has been working in the building & home repair services field for 17 years. He has a Home Improvement Contractors License, Construction Supervisors License and is fully insured so that your property is protected!
